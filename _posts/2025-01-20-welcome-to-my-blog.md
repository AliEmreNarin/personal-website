Writing is a way of thinking out loud. I've kept notes and half-formed ideas scattered across notebooks and text files for years, but I've never had a single place to put them where they might be useful to someone else.

That changes here. This blog has two tracks:

**Technical** posts will cover things like research ideas, software, tools, and methods. I'll try to write about things I'm actually working on rather than abstract surveys.

**Non-technical** posts will be everything else — books, ideas, observations, things I find interesting and can't stop thinking about.

I'm not aiming for a consistent schedule or a particular audience. I'm writing to clarify my own thinking, and if something is useful to you, that's a bonus.

Let's see where this goes.
